About McDonald's

McDonald's is the world's leading global foodservice retailer with over 38,000 locations in over 100 countries. We serve approximately 69 million customers daily, providing them with high-quality food and exceptional service at an affordable price.

Our mission is to make delicious, feel-good moments easy for everyone. We are committed to serving responsibly sourced, sustainably raised, and ethically treated ingredients. At McDonald's, we believe in making a positive impact on the communities we serve and the planet we share.

Join us in our journey to create smiles and memories, one meal at a time.
